Signal PWA

Notifications

Enable push notifications to receive alerts when new messages arrive.

Instructions

  1. Tap "Enable Notifications" above
  2. Grant notification permission when prompted
  3. Add to Home Screen (iOS: Share > Add to Home Screen)
  4. Send a message using signal-cli
  5. Receive push notification on this device

Note: Your device must be online to receive notifications. Messages are stored locally on your laptop - this PWA connects over Tailscale.
